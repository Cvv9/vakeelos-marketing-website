# VakeelOS — Feature Video Prompts

> **Target generators:** Sora, Runway Gen-3 Alpha, Kling
> **Style:** Modern dark-mode SaaS UI. No people, no hands, no courtrooms.
> **Palette:** Deep charcoal background (`#111`), saffron-gold accent (`#C9962A`), off-white body text.
> **Audience:** New-age law firms and independent advocates — not old-school legal.
> **Motion rule:** All transitions under 300ms. Easing: `cubic-bezier(0.25, 0.46, 0.45, 0.94)`. No camera shake, no zoom.
> **Append to every prompt for Sora/Kling:** `"cinematic quality, 4K, no motion blur, consistent dark UI"`
> **Runway Gen-3 tip:** Break each act into a separate 4-second clip, stitch in post with the specified transition type. Or use image-to-video from a static frame for maximum layout control.
